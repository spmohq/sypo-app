How to set up your content distribution channel:

  1. LM - PDF
  2. Hosting of LP - Graphy.com
    • As it gives you the opportunity to capture the emails of the downloaders. You can add them to your Google Contacts and import to your autoresponder.
    • For sequence - you can use the 'send later' feature of Gmail.
    • For broadcast - you will use your autoresponder.
  3. LCP - dedicated domain, repo and your LM's "Lead Capture Page's Copy".
  4. For other contents, to drive traffic to your LCP, you need two things:
    1. Persona of Your LM's audience
    2. Pain Points etc 4 emotions of Your LM's audience
